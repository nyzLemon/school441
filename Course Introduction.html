Employment prospects

1. Network Engineer

Mainly responsible for network construction, management, and maintenance of enterprises, organizations, or institutions, ensuring network availability and security, and able to configure equipment and network planning according to specific needs

2. network administrator

Responsible for monitoring and maintaining network systems, ensuring their normal and stable operation, and resolving network failures and security issues

3. Network Security Expert

Use various technologies and means to manage and maintain network security, prevent network attacks and data theft, and establish security policies and prevention systems

4. Wireless Network Engineer

Mainly responsible for the construction, management, and maintenance of wireless networks for enterprises or organizations, designing and optimizing wireless coverage, ensuring the availability and security of wireless networks

5. Autonomous Traffic Network Controller

Responsible for monitoring and managing autonomous transportation network systems, coordinating station and train scheduling.
